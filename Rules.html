Rules

  1. Writing something of book is not allowed
  2. All books are free of cost for 3 days.
  3. If you want to give book for 3 days you have to pay Rs:20 per day on each book.
  4. All books are available for maximum 7 days.
  5. If you not return the book with in days which you have select than you have to pay Rs:50 per day.
  6. If you destroy any book than you have to pay book price with Rs:200 charge within book return days otherwise you have to pay 50 rupees per day.
  7. If you destroy book and not want to pay fine you have to provide new book within return days otherwise you have to pay 50 rupees per day.
  8. If you get your book from library there are no charges of it.
  9. If you want to get books at home you have to pay 50 rupees for book delivery and 50 rupees for book return.
